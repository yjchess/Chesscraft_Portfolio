Creative Design

Chesscraft uses a variety of different softwares and hardwares to create pieces of art. These range from 3d models, 2d digital art and 2d traditional art. On top of this, chesscraft also creates art through the use of miniatures and other non-conventional methods.

Unfortunately, due to the nature of Chesscraft's financial situation, he currently only works with free software and hardware. Due to personal circumstances, he has also lost access to his graphics tablet. If you would like to help Chesscraft delve deeper into his passion for Creative Design, please consider donating.
