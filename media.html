General structure

The process with which the majority of Chesscraft's videos are recorded are as follows:

  1. Plan Video [set up powerpoint slides, scripts and software]
  2. Record Video in OBS / StreamLabs OBS
  3. Import to Shotcut
  4. Edit audio, video, effects
  5. Add in effects, images + Other from websites
  6. Find/Create Suitable thumbnail
  7. Upload to Youtube
